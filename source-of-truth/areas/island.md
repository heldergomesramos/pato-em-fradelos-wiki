# Areas — The Island Surface

---

## The Island

Remote, previously unknown island. Discovered via satellite detecting black anomalies. Currently appears abandoned. The surface features water areas with lily pads (nenúfares) — the early-game zone where the player begins.

A new **wet/green cave biome** is planned below the surface, between the island exterior and the underground lab.

---

## The Black Lake

A lake on the island surface, located in an open mountain valley between two mountains — exposed to sky. Entirely solidified by Black Matter — hollow inside. Was the **entry point for the original research team**.

- **Fireflies** can escape the Black Lake; other creatures cannot
- The interior is traversable — one of the first major BM hollow spaces the player encounters
- Lore significance: the team entered through here, which is also how BM first spread to the facility

**Eclipse interaction:** When The Stranger reaches zenith at midnight, the Black Lake is struck by direct eclipse shadow. PE tracked Pofinho's gradual return to consciousness by reading BM patterns in the lake during eclipses.

**Mid-to-late game threshold:** The Black Lake is the literal threshold between mid-game and the final stretch. Entering it during the eclipse and exiting through the right side before the moon returns unlocks the path to the observatory. Technically discoverable early, practically reached once the player knows to try it.

A deep fissure beneath the lake connects underground to the BM generator inside the mountain. The lake is the **reservoir**, the generator is the **engine**, the fissure is the **conduit**.

**Open question:** What else is in the Black Lake interior beyond the keycard room? What makes it worth returning to?
