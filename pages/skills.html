// Movement System //

Skills

The Duck has a unique set of abilities that define its playstyle. Each skill opens new possibilities for exploration and combat.

SKILL_01
Dash
Quick Horizontal Burst

An energy burst that propels the Duck in a direction in a fraction of a second. While dashing, the Duck briefly becomes intangible — projectiles and attacks pass through without dealing damage. Essential for dodging, repositioning, and reaching distant platforms.

Movement Invincible Recharges on Ground
SHIFT
+ direction
SKILL_02
Wall Slide
Wall Slide + Wall Jump

When approaching a wall while falling, the Duck automatically grabs it and slides slowly downward instead of dropping. From this position, you can jump with full momentum in the opposite direction, gaining altitude to reach higher platforms or chain movements.

Platforming Climbing Wall Jump
↑
when touching the wall
SKILL_03
Purple Plant
Gloamvine Projectile

The Duck launches a concentrated Gloamvine seed charged with Black Matter. Upon hitting a surface or enemy, the seed explodes into vines that entangle and temporarily immobilise targets. Trapped creatures become vulnerable to follow-up attacks with increased damage.

Projectile Entangles Enemies Black Matter Increased Damage
E
throw plant
SKILL_04
Shadow Recall
Shadow Recall — Teleport

The Duck can mark a shadow point on the map. At any moment, with a single press, it instantly returns to that point — phasing through walls, enemies, and hazards without interaction. The shadow appears on the ground as a purple marker. Ideal for escaping critical situations or repositioning strategically.

Teleport Phases Through Walls Marker Escape
Q
mark / teleport
SKILL_05
Glide
Aerial Glide

The Duck spreads its wings and glides through the air, maintaining altitude and controlling the descent with precision. While gliding, the fall is extremely slow and the Duck can travel great horizontal distances. Allows reaching platforms that would be impossible with a normal jump, and can be chained with an aerial dash.

Aerial Fall Control Distance Combo with Dash
ESPAÇO
hold in the air
