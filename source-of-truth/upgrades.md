# Upgrades & Abilities

---

## Current Upgrades

### Dash
Unlocks Dash, Sprint, and Dive simultaneously.
- **Dash:** Horizontal burst movement
- **Dash + S:** Dash downward
- **Dash + S (hold) — Dive:** At surface, increases max fall speed
- **Underwater Dash:** Single use; goes forward or downward only

> **Design note:** Dive is not a separate upgrade. It is available as soon as Dash is acquired. Locking content behind Dive is technically possible but not a strong gate — Dash comes very early in the game.

### Wall Jump
Stick to walls and jump off them.

### Gloamvine Core
Shoots sticky glowing purple balls from the duck's mouth. Strong light source.
- Reveals BM interior contents: creature roots, hidden creatures, Lurker outlines
- Max **5 charges**, regenerate **1 every 3 seconds**
- Balls stick to surfaces and persist in the environment
- Used as a light weapon against Shadow and Shadow Wisps
- **Reveals hidden engravings** when thrown into a room — key to finding the deep portal coordinates

## Planned Upgrades

### Dark Duck — Confirmed

Acquired by touching the **BM Seed** at the bottom of the **Last Abyss** — the origin point of all Black Matter. The upgrade is not a designed mechanism: a self-observing BM entity touching the origin of all BM unlocks something in his nature. Full control of the matter inside him.

- **Toggle:** Q key (repurposes the Quack, which previously did nothing useful)
- **What it does:** Suppresses Pofinho's internal light entirely. Allows passing through BM that his light would otherwise make solid and visible to him.
- **Hard limitation:** Cannot use the Gloamvine Core while Dark Duck is active. Internal light is fully suppressed — shooting light balls is impossible. Forces meaningful choice in puzzles.
- **No time limit** — acquired late game; player has seen most content.
- **Lore note:** Do NOT add flavor text about quacking or connect it to Pofinho's personality. Pofinho was not always a duck. Q is mechanically elegant, not lore-significant.

> The original Conceal upgrade (actively toggle black as an activated ability) was discarded — it didn't respect the design principle of upgrades having interesting multiple functionalities, and felt arbitrary. Dark Duck replaces it naturally, earned through gameplay at a specific late-game location.

→ See `areas/depths.md` for the Last Abyss and BM Seed location  
→ See `duck_lore.md` for lore basis (Pofinho as self-observing BM entity)
