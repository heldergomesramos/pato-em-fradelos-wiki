# Sleep Mechanic

---

## What It Does

- Speeds time up to **150x**
- Stops the duck from observing — BM platforms the duck is on or near will vanish
- Used for time-based puzzles and traversal through BM
- **Key mechanic for accessing The Stranger:** sleeping inside an active Teleporter Sphere enters The Stranger; sleeping again exits to the intended destination

## Lore Basis

Crossing Black Matter requires full unconsciousness. Closing eyes alone is insufficient — light still enters through eyelids. True sleep or full unconsciousness is the reliable method.

**Sleeping does not dissolve Pofinho.** The stabilizer ore maintains his physical form passively even when conscious attention is elsewhere. Self-observation is a property of his BM — an internalized observer function — not something actively performed every second. Sleep interrupts neither. He stays solid and can die to hazards while asleep.

This is why the Sleep mechanic is meaningful: it's not just time-skip, it's the duck fully ceasing to actively observe — while his body remains present.

---

## Connection to The Stranger

Sleep is how Pofinho accesses The Stranger's interior via Teleporter Spheres. When he sleeps mid-transit, he becomes temporarily indistinguishable from unobserved BM — the teleporter loses its anchor and cannot pull him back out. He remains inside The Stranger until he wakes up. Sleeping again exits to the intended real-world destination.

No time passes outside during time spent in The Stranger, regardless of how long the player spends inside.
