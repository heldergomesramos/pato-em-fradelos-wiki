_Items resolved this session:_

- **Is Pofinho confirmed as the consciousness in the duck?** → Yes. Confirmed working canon. See `duck_lore.md`.
- **Why a duck specifically?** → Practical (ducks were on the island) + emotional (Pofinho was a remote observer; chose a body built to move through the world). No dialogue needed. See `duck_lore.md`.
- **What is the satisfying ending?** → Core ending: Pofinho merges with The Stranger's core, stabilizes it. Observatory is the revelation, not the ending. Incomplete stopping point replaces the "alternate" ending. Third layer deferred. See `duck_lore.md` and `areas/the_stranger.md`.
- **What is Internull?** → The scientists' name for The Stranger's interior, named before they understood what The Stranger was. Quiet dramatic irony. See `areas/internull.md`.
- **How does teleportation work?** → Routes traveler through The Stranger. Sleep mid-transit = remain inside. Two-axis destination system (portal coordinates × Stranger orbital position). See `areas/internull.md`.
- **Is The Stranger the origin of BM or a casualty?** → Origin. An ancient planet consumed by BM, now slowly dispersing itself. See `areas/the_stranger.md`.
- **Can you reach The Stranger via Teleporter?** → Yes, via Sleep + Teleporter. Core requires the hidden deep portal + eclipse. See `areas/internull.md`.
- **What is the real threat?** → Deep ocean BM spread in permanent darkness, accelerating without observation to collapse it back. Surface island spread is the visible indicator. See `mechanics/black_matter.md`.
- **Why doesn't the world mobilize?** → The team reported it; they weren't believed. Institutional inertia, no physical proof anyone could safely examine, the people who knew disappeared. See `mechanics/black_matter.md`.
- **Is The Observer fully artificial or does it contain a human consciousness?** → Intentionally ambiguous. Both interpretations left open. See `creatures/bosses.md`.
- **Post-Teleporter Blackout mechanic** → Removed. Superseded by Sleep + Teleporter = enter The Stranger.
- **Conceal upgrade as designed** → Set aside. Needs redesign. See `upgrades.md`.
- **Who stayed at the observatory?** → PE confirmed. See `areas/observatory.md` and `scientists.md`.
- **PE's fate** → Died at the observatory before Pofinho arrived. See `areas/observatory.md`.
- **Does sleeping dissolve Pofinho?** → No. Stabilizer ore maintains form. Self-observation is internalized, not actively performed. See `duck_lore.md` and `mechanics/observation.md`.
- **BM infection mechanics** → Treating a wound makes it worse (collapses a more-spread state into existence). See `mechanics/black_matter.md`.
