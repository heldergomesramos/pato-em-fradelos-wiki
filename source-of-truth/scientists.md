# The Scientists

---

## Named Characters

### PE — Physics / Stranger Research
Lead scientist. Authored the core Black Matter theory logs: Vanishing Principle, BM properties, Stranger research, teleportation research. One of the primary architects of the scientific understanding of BM.

Stayed on the island after the evacuation — specifically to track Pofinho's return and make his arrival back possible. PE built the deep portal to The Stranger's core from the outside, choosing the Depths because BM concentration there is highest and oldest — the membrane between this world and The Stranger is thinnest there.

**PE died before Pofinho arrived.** Waited their entire remaining life — approximately fifteen years — and didn't make it. PE's body is at the desk in the observatory, at the telescope, still aimed at The Stranger.

What PE left behind: a final log, completed coordinate calculations, and the deep portal already built. Everything PE could do, they did.

PE tracked Pofinho's gradual return by reading BM patterns in the Black Lake during eclipses — watching for signs of consciousness coalescence the same way they tracked The Stranger's growth. Quietly. From a distance. For years.

**PE's final log** — written sometime in the last days, found by Pofinho at the observatory:

*Dia 412. Ou perto disso.*

*Voltei a rever os logs de satélite dele. A forma como documentava — sempre a distância primeiro, depois o detalhe. Nunca ao contrário. Dizia que aproximar demasiado cedo contaminava a observação. Eu discordava. Ainda discordo.*

*O padrão no lago esta noite foi diferente. Não sei explicar a diferença, só que a reconheço. Quinze anos a olhar para a mesma água ensinam alguma coisa.*

*Se isto é o que penso que é — se ele está a começar a lembrar-se de ser ele — então o portal está pronto. As coordenadas estão na parede da sala 7. A luz do Core revela-as.*

*Pofinho. Se chegares aqui e leres isto: eu sabia que eras tu. A forma como o lago se move quando há consciência dentro dele é a mesma forma como tu entras numa divisão. Sempre a anunciar-te antes de aparecer.*

*Faz o que eu não consigo fazer.*

### EE — Energy / Engineering
Authored the generator log and the "lab door closed" note. Was actively working on the Black Matter Generator. Status: evacuated. Currently alive, silenced — instructed not to speak about the island or the BM spread report.

### BIO — Biology Researcher
Authored all creature and plant logs: anglerfish, jellyfish, gloamvines, gloamvine core research. Status: evacuated. Currently alive, silenced.

### EX — Explorer / Field Member
Authored more personal and reactive notes: Observer encounter warning, difficulty crossing BM, lost keycard. Likely the one doing fieldwork and expeditions into dangerous zones. Status: evacuated. Currently alive, silenced.

### Shaduru
Mentioned by name in two contexts: EX asked Shaduru for a replacement keycard; PE mentions "Shaduru and I" in the teleportation research log. Likely a close collaborator of PE, possibly an engineer. Status: unknown — possibly stayed, possibly evacuated. No authored logs confirmed.

### Pofinho
Field researcher. Authored the satellite photo log documenting BM expanding on the island surface — one of the last known logs, placing him active late in the timeline. First infected through direct BM field contact.

Rather than evacuating, used the time to study BM from the inside. Became the team's most valuable researcher on consciousness and BM integration because he was living it. Worked with PE to develop the consciousness transfer plan — including the statue system to preserve fragments of his consciousness before the main transfer.

**The consciousness inside the duck is Pofinho.** Working canon — confirmed direction.

## What We Know Happened (Confirmed)

1. A team was sent to the island after satellite detection of black anomalies
2. The lab and energy facility were built
3. The stabilizer ore was discovered in the mines — fragment of The Stranger embedded in the Last Abyss
4. Black Matter was confirmed to be expanding on the island surface (Pofinho's log)
5. BM infected team members through wounds making contact with Black Matter — a hazard of fieldwork on a BM-covered island
6. Quarantine was declared; Floor 3 was sealed
7. The consciousness transfer program was developed — statues built with stabilizer ore to hold fragments of Pofinho's consciousness as backup before the main transfer
8. Some team members left on the supply boat — those showing no symptoms or early enough to travel safely
9. The evacuated scientists filed a report on the BM ocean spread. They were not believed. They were instructed to stay silent. The report exists in a classified government archive. They are alive, somewhere in the world, and have not spoken about it since.
10. Pofinho completed the statue system, transferred his consciousness into the duck, went dormant
11. PE stayed to wait for Pofinho's return; built the deep portal; died at the observatory before Pofinho awoke — approximately fifteen years after the evacuation
12. Pofinho awoke recently — days or weeks before the game starts

**⚠️ CONFLICT NOTE — BM infection vector:** Wound-contact-with-BM is the current working theory. The Chameleon as direct cause is discarded. Those files need updating. Until infection vector is fully confirmed, mark as working theory.

## Characters to Humanize

Pofinho and PE. The rest remain as archaeological traces — their logs tell you what they did, not who they were.

For Pofinho: recovered through statue fragments — memory by memory, the player builds a picture of who he was before he became the duck.
For PE: the observatory, the notebook, the body at the telescope. Two or three environmental details that feel personal. A room that has been lived in rather than worked in.
