# Areas — Internull (The Other Dimension)

> Working name: Internull. Where Black Matter goes when it vanishes.

---

## Teleporter Spheres

Large black spheres found in the Energy Facility (and potentially elsewhere). Mechanism:
- Enter the sphere
- Input coordinates
- Exit from another sphere at the destination

**Interior:** Completely black. Standing inside means you cannot see out, and outside observers cannot see in. This creates the exact conditions for BM's vanishing principle — you disappear from the normal dimension and traverse Internull to re-emerge at the destination.

The Depths Chamber is accessed by a creative use of a Teleporter Sphere's mechanics — using momentum and recalling to slam through the Aquarium glass. → See `areas/depths.md`

---

## Internull — Properties

Where BM goes when unobserved. A dimension that exists parallel to the normal world.

| Property | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| Time | Behaves differently — may not pass at all without consciousness to observe it |
| Access | Teleporter Spheres, possibly Sleep, possibly the Conceal upgrade |
| Traversal | Conscious beings (the duck, The Observer) can move through it |
| Recording | A camera sent through recorded nothing — no time passed inside during its transit |

**Theory:** Time in Internull requires observation/consciousness to "render." A camera is not conscious, so no time passed for it. The duck is conscious, so it can traverse meaningfully.

---

## Sleep & Internull

Sleep may allow partial or full access to Internull. Candidates:
- Sleeping inside a Teleporter Sphere = travel directly to Internull
- The duck's consciousness partially phases into Internull during sleep, which is why BM vanishes around it — the BM is following the duck's consciousness partway

→ See `mechanics/sleep.md`

---

## Conceal & Internull

The planned Conceal upgrade (turn fully black, become undetectable) may allow phasing into/out of BM at will — essentially controlled Internull access without a Teleporter.
