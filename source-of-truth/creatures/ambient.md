# Creatures — Ambient & Wildlife

---

## Anglerfish

Black. Live inside Black Matter. Use a **fake sticky glowing flower as bait** — mimics gloamvine flowers to lure fireflies and curious explorers. First discovered when a team member nearly lost a hand reaching for what appeared to be a real flower.

Found only within or partially within BM. Cannot fully leave it.

---

## Jellyfish

Black. Live inside Black Matter. Can swim despite no water being present inside BM — the mechanism for this is unexplained and intentionally alien. Found deep within BM zones.

---

## Fireflies

Small yellow glowing creatures. Found both inside and outside BM. Drawn to light sources. Possibly trapped inside BM when light sealed it — they were following the light and got caught. Theorized to be prey of the Anglerfish.

**Notable:** Fireflies are the only creatures confirmed to be able to escape the Black Lake. All others remain trapped inside.

---

## Black Whale

Enormous. Lives in the Last Abyss below the lab and everything else. **Insta-kills the duck** if it falls deep enough into the abyss.

Invisible until it's too late — consistent with BM creatures being black and hard to detect in darkness. Its presence is meant to make the abyss feel alive and dangerous rather than just a void.
