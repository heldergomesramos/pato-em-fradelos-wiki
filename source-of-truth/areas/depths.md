# Areas — The Depths

---

## The Depths Chamber

A sub-zone directly below the Aquarium. First hinted at early in the game via the thick glass panel in the Aquarium floor — the player sees total darkness below but cannot interact with it yet. It sits in memory, foreshadowing.

### How to Access
Requires the Teleporter. The sequence:
1. Use momentum from the Facility corridor's launch platform
2. At the peak of the jump, drop the Teleporter shadow
3. Recall while positioned below the Aquarium glass
4. Duck slams through the panel — the crack becomes a permanent entrance

### What's Inside
- **The Shadow** — boss. Chase sequence. Defeated by voluntarily extinguishing all light at a specific moment.
- **Lurkers** — flat 2D enemies living on surfaces. Steal Gloamvine Core charges.
- **Shadow Wisps** — small undeveloped Shadow entities. Teach "darkness = safety" before the boss fight.
- Unique environment teaching the player that darkness is protection, not just obstacle.

### The Deep Portal
The hidden portal to The Stranger's core is located here — in the Depths, not below the Black Lake. PE chose this location because BM concentration here is highest and oldest. The membrane between this world and The Stranger is thinnest here. PE built the door where the wall was already weakest.

Finding the portal requires the correct coordinates (hidden in wall engravings, revealed by the Gloamvine Core) and the eclipse at midnight. Sleeping inside the deep portal during the eclipse sends Pofinho to The Stranger's core.

## The Last Abyss

A near-infinite black pit below the lab and everything else. Not a designed zone — it's the void beneath the world.

**Contains:**
- **Black Whale** — enormous, insta-kills the duck if it falls deep enough. Invisible until it's too late.
- Anglerfish and jellyfish in the upper layers
- Evokes fear of the unknown depth — the player should never feel safe falling here

The Last Abyss is intentionally not a zone you "complete." It's a boundary and a threat.
