# The Scientists

---

## Named Characters

### PE — Physics / Stranger Research
Lead scientist. Authored the core Black Matter theory logs: Vanishing Principle, BM properties, Stranger research, teleportation research. One of the primary architects of the scientific understanding of BM.

Stayed on the island after the evacuation — specifically to track Pofinho's return and make his arrival back possible. PE built the deep portal to The Stranger's core from the outside, choosing the Depths because BM concentration there is highest and oldest — the membrane between this world and The Stranger is thinnest there.

**PE died before Pofinho arrived.** Waited their entire remaining life and didn't make it. PE's body is at the desk in the observatory, at the telescope, still aimed at The Stranger.

What PE left behind: a final log, completed coordinate calculations, and the deep portal already built. Everything PE could do, they did.

PE tracked Pofinho's gradual return by reading BM patterns in the Black Lake during eclipses — watching for signs of consciousness coalescence the same way they tracked The Stranger's growth. Quietly. From a distance. For years.

→ See `areas/observatory.md` for observatory details and PE's final log

### EE — Energy / Engineering
Authored the generator log and the "lab door closed" note. Was actively working on the Black Matter Generator. Status: unknown — probably left on the evacuation boat.

### BIO — Biology Researcher
Authored all creature and plant logs: anglerfish, jellyfish, gloamvines, gloamvine core research. Status: unknown — probably left.

### EX — Explorer / Field Member
Authored more personal and reactive notes: Observer encounter warning, difficulty crossing BM, lost keycard. Likely the one doing fieldwork and expeditions into dangerous zones. Status: unknown — probably left.

### Shaduru
Mentioned by name in two contexts: EX asked Shaduru for a replacement keycard; PE mentions "Shaduru and I" in the teleportation research log. Likely a close collaborator of PE, possibly an engineer. Status: unknown — possibly stayed, possibly left. No authored logs confirmed.

### Pofinho
Field researcher. Authored the satellite photo log documenting BM expanding on the island surface — one of the last known logs, placing him active late in the timeline. First infected through direct BM field contact.

Rather than evacuating, used the time to study BM from the inside. Became the team's most valuable researcher on consciousness and BM integration because he was living it. Transferred his consciousness into a BM construct (the duck) before full corruption.

**The consciousness inside the duck is Pofinho.** Working canon — confirmed direction.

→ See `duck_lore.md` for the full self-observing entity theory and lore

## What We Know Happened (Confirmed)

1. A team was sent to the island after satellite detection of black anomalies
2. The lab and energy facility were built
3. Black Matter was confirmed to be expanding on the island surface (Pofinho's log)
4. BM infected team members through wounds making contact with Black Matter — a hazard of fieldwork on a BM-covered island
5. Quarantine was declared; Floor 3 was sealed
6. Some team members left on the supply boat — those showing no symptoms or early enough to travel safely. Those who left carry the knowledge and tell no one: infected, and any contaminated material they brought back would spread BM
7. Pofinho stayed, completed his research, transferred his consciousness into the duck
8. PE stayed to wait for Pofinho's return; built the deep portal; died at the observatory before Pofinho awoke
9. Pofinho awoke recently — days or weeks before the game starts

**⚠️ CONFLICT NOTE — BM infection vector:** The session notes discard the Chameleon as the direct cause of infection and mark wound-contact-with-BM as the current working theory. This contradicts `bosses.md` and `laboratory.md`, which currently state the Chameleon's attack caused wounds that let BM into bloodstreams. The Chameleon may still be present on Floor 3 as a creature, but it is no longer the cause of the infection outbreak. Those files need updating. Until infection vector is fully confirmed, mark as working theory in both places.
