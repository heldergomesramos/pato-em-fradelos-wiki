# Areas — The Research Laboratory

> Multi-floor underground facility built beneath the mountains of the island.

---

## Overview

Built by the research team after establishing a base on the island. Houses creature research, energy generation study, BM theory work, and The Stranger research. Now partially quarantined and abandoned.

---

## Floors & Rooms

### Creature Research Floors
Dedicated rooms per creature species. Used to study BM creatures in controlled conditions.

### The Aquarium
Large chamber simulating a natural habitat for observing creatures. Contains **The Observer** — a BM entity created artificially to keep creatures "locked in existence" by observing them.

The Aquarium floor has a **thick glass panel** showing total darkness below. The player can see it early but cannot interact with it — it foreshadows the Depths Chamber. Access is gained later via the Teleporter.

→ See `creatures/bosses.md` for The Observer
→ See `areas/depths.md` for the Depths Chamber

### Gloamvine Research Room
Dedicated to studying black gloamvines. **Warning: 3 are growing uncontrollably.** Physical distortion zones present.

→ See `creatures/plants.md`

### The Stranger Floor
A floor dedicated entirely to researching The Stranger planet. PE's primary workspace for the Stranger theory logs.

→ See `areas/the_stranger.md`

### Kinetic Energy Room
Contains the machine that gives the duck the **Dash upgrade**.

### Floor 3 — QUARANTINED
Permanently sealed. Accessible only from Floor 2 via a black gloamvine's physical distortion zone — no normal entrance exists. Contains:
- Dead scientists
- Blood
- The Chameleon (still present, uncontained)

Sealed after the Chameleon incident. The Chameleon escaped here, attacked team members, and caused BM contamination through wounds entering the bloodstream. The quarantine that followed shut down parts of the facility and led to some team members evacuating.

→ See `creatures/bosses.md` for The Chameleon
→ See `scientists.md` for the quarantine timeline
