# The Scientists

---

## Named Characters

### PE — Physics / Environment Researcher
Lead scientist. Authored the core Black Matter theory logs: Vanishing Principle, BM properties, Stranger research, teleportation research. One of the primary architects of the scientific understanding of BM.

### EE — Energy / Engineering
Authored the generator log and the "lab door closed" note. Was actively working on the Black Matter Generator.

### BIO — Biology Researcher
Authored all creature and plant logs: anglerfish, jellyfish, gloamvines, gloamvine core research.

### EX — Explorer / Field Member
Authored more personal and reactive notes: Observer encounter warning, difficulty crossing BM, lost keycard. Likely the one doing fieldwork and expeditions into dangerous zones.

### Shaduru
Mentioned by name in two contexts: EX asked Shaduru for a replacement keycard; PE mentions "Shaduru and I" in the teleportation research log. Likely a close collaborator of PE, possibly an engineer. No authored logs confirmed.

### Pofinho
Authored the satellite photo log documenting BM expanding on the island surface. This is one of the last known logs, placing him active late in the timeline. **Leading candidate for the consciousness inside the duck.** His name appears in both in-game logs and design notes as the primary candidate.

→ See `duck_lore.md` for the full Pofinho theory

## What We Know Happened (Confirmed)

1. A team was sent to the island after satellite detection of black anomalies
2. The lab and energy facility were built
3. Black Matter was confirmed to be expanding on the island surface (Pofinho's log)
4. The Chameleon escaped on Floor 3 and attacked team members — BM entered bloodstreams via wounds
5. Quarantine was declared; Floor 3 was sealed with dead scientists inside
6. Parts of the facility were shut down; some team members left the island
7. At least one member stayed and transferred their consciousness into the duck
