# Upgrades & Abilities

---

## Current Upgrades

### Dash
Unlocks Dash, Sprint, and Dive simultaneously.
- **Dash:** Horizontal burst movement
- **Dash + S:** Dash downward
- **Dash + S (hold) — Dive:** At surface, increases max fall speed
- **Underwater Dash:** Single use; goes forward or downward only

### Wall Jump
Stick to walls and jump off them.

### Gloamvine Core
Shoots sticky glowing purple balls from the duck's mouth. Strong light source.
- Reveals BM interior contents: creature roots, hidden creatures, Lurker outlines
- Max **5 charges**, regenerate **1 every 3 seconds**
- Balls stick to surfaces and persist in the environment
- Used as a light weapon against Shadow and Shadow Wisps
- **Reveals hidden engravings** when thrown into a room — key to finding the deep portal coordinates

## Planned Upgrades

### Conceal — Unresolved Design

The original Conceal upgrade concept (actively turn black, become undetectable, possibly phase through BM) is set aside. It doesn't respect the design principle of upgrades having interesting multiple functionalities, and feels arbitrary as an activated ability.

**Better directions to explore:**
- Turning black as a **side effect** of something else — spending too long inside BM zones, the stabilizer ore degrading temporarily, a state the player enters rather than activates
- Turning black as an **environmental interaction** — a specific substance or area causes it
- Keeping the concept (Pofinho can revert to raw BM) but finding a mechanic that earns it through gameplay

**Status: unresolved. Needs a dedicated design session.**

The reward for passing The Observer (coordinates to a new location) is still valid and may be decoupled from any "Conceal" mechanic.

→ See `duck_lore.md` for lore basis (Pofinho as self-observing BM entity)
