# Creatures — Plants & Vines

---

## Gloamvines (Normal)

Green vines with **purple sticky glowing flowers** at the bottom. Grow from grassy ceilings and dark places. The duck's three tongues can grab these flowers for traversal. They're also what the Anglerfish mimics.

---

## Black Gloamvines

Gloamvines that have contacted Black Matter. Distinct from normal gloamvines in both appearance and properties.

**Properties:**
- Always grow from ceilings that contain Black Matter
- Roots are **invisible** — surrounded by pure BM, only visible under strong Gloamvine Core light
- Cause **physical distortion**: the material they grow through appears solid, but their intersection point does not physically exist — objects and characters can pass through

**Known design issue:** Interaction with spikes above/below a physical distortion zone is unresolved. If the duck passes through distortion, what happens if spikes exist on the other side? TBD fix.

The Gloamvine Research Room in the lab is dedicated to studying these. Three are growing uncontrollably there.

Floor 3 of the lab is accessible only via a Black Gloamvine's distortion zone — no normal entrance exists.

→ See `mechanics/black_matter.md` for physical distortion rules

## Carnivorous Plants

Black. Found fully or partially within BM. Roots are invisible (surrounded by pure BM) — visible only with a strong enough light source like the Gloamvine Core. Passive unless the duck enters their range.
