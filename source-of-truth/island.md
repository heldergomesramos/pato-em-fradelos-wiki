# Areas — The Island Surface

---

## The Island

Remote, previously unknown island. Discovered via satellite detecting black anomalies. Currently appears abandoned. The surface features water areas with lily pads (nenúfares) — this is the early-game zone where the player begins.

A new **wet/green cave biome** is planned below the surface, between the island exterior and the underground lab.

---

## The Black Lake

A lake on the island surface, located above the lab, between two mountains. Entirely solidified by Black Matter — hollow inside. Was the **entry point for the original research team**.

- **Fireflies** can escape the Black Lake; other creatures cannot
- The interior is traversable — it's one of the first major BM hollow spaces the player encounters
- Lore significance: the team entered through here, which is also how BM first spread to the facility via contact with team members
