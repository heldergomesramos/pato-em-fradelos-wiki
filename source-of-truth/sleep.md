# Sleep Mechanic

---

## What It Does

- Speeds time up to **150x**
- Stops the duck from observing — BM platforms the duck is on or near will vanish
- Used for time-based puzzles and traversal through BM

## Lore Basis

Crossing Black Matter requires full unconsciousness. Closing eyes alone is insufficient — light still enters through eyelids. True sleep or full unconsciousness is the reliable method.

This is why the Sleep upgrade is mechanically meaningful: it's not just time-skip, it's the duck fully ceasing to observe.

---

## Connection to Internull

Sleep may be the duck's method of accessing the Other Dimension (Internull). Candidates:
- Sleeping inside a Teleporter Sphere = travel to Internull directly
- The duck's consciousness partially phases into Internull during sleep (explaining why BM vanishes — it's following the duck's consciousness partway)

After using a large Teleporter Sphere, the duck temporarily turns fully black and can traverse BM without sleeping. This would be a brief window enabling unique puzzle sequences. Duration TBD.
