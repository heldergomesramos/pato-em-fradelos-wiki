# Areas — The Stranger

---

## What It Is

A large black planet orbiting between Earth and the Moon, roughly moon-sized. Detected by the research team's instruments. Has a dedicated research floor in the lab (PE's primary domain).

---

## Known Properties

- Causes a **total eclipse every 3 days at midnight** — complete darkness on the island
- A scout camera sent to its surface **phased through it** — consistent with the surface being made of Black Matter
- No light reflects off it — it absorbs everything, same as BM

---

## Leading Theory

The Stranger is an ancient moon or planet entirely consumed by Black Matter — possibly the **origin point of all Black Matter**. An alternative: it was a casualty of BM, not its source — a planet that was infected and consumed, same as what may happen to Earth.

Which is true (origin vs. casualty) is unresolved and may be a central lore question the game never fully answers — consistent with the Outer Wilds influence.

---

## Eclipse Mechanic Implications

Every 3 days at midnight, the island experiences total darkness. If this is ever used as a gameplay mechanic:
- All BM on the surface would vanish during the eclipse (unobserved in total darkness)
- This could open normally-blocked paths or create unique traversal windows
- Currently not confirmed as a gameplay mechanic — TBD

---

## Can You Reach It?

Unresolved. A Teleporter Sphere could theoretically target The Stranger's coordinates. What's inside — if anything — is a major open question.
