# Areas — Energy Facility & Extraction Corridor

---

## The Energy Facility

Located to the right of the lab, connected by a metal bridge. The industrial heart of the operation.

**Contains:**
- **Black Matter Generator** — processes BM into energy, powers the lab and all teleporters. Harvests stored light energy from BM. Authored by EE.
- **Rail-mounted metal boxes** transporting light-reflecting crystals from the mining sites via the Extraction Corridor
- **Lasers** — gameplay-functional, lore TBD. Candidates: cutting BM, treating BM, infusing concentrated light into BM
- **A Teleporter Sphere**
- **A boss** — creature/entity TBD, reward TBD

→ See `areas/internull.md` for Teleporter Sphere mechanics
→ See `open_questions.md` for the laser lore question and boss TBD

## The Mining Sites

Two sites located near the lab. Extract **light-reflecting crystals** from the rock. These crystals are:
- Transported to the facility via the Extraction Corridor
- Used in the Black Matter Generator
- Used in light-redirection puzzle mechanics throughout the facility

---

## The Extraction Corridor

Industrial transition zone between the Energy Facility and the wind zone. The zone where the facility's rail system is most prominent and puzzle-central.

**Features:**
- Corrupted metal containers (same as in the facility) moving along rails
- Some containers pass through unlit zones — **if a container enters darkness, it vanishes from under the duck** unless the Gloamvine Core is active
- Core tension: **"observe or fall"** — the player must maintain light on the container or fall through it
- Light-reflecting crystal puzzles introduced here
- Lore justification: containers run from mining sites to the facility carrying crystals

**Key mechanic introduced:** BM-constructed containers disappear in darkness, teaching the player that actively maintaining light on BM surfaces is a survival requirement — not just a puzzle tool.
